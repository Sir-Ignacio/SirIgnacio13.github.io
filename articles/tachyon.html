What Is A Tachyon?

It is a well established fact in the physics community that light is the fastest particle in the universe, however how would a particle behave if it were faster than the speed of light? The tachyon comes from the greek word, τάχυον, which means fast when translated. The more energy the tachyon loses, then the faster it travles. Although it may sound complicated, the math coincides with Albert Einstein's General Theory of Relativity.
